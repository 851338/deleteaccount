Delete Your Mobi Chill Account

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from the Mobi Chill app, please follow the steps below.

Step 1: Send an email to tvndbhk@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete My Account

Please include your registered email address or user ID (if applicable) in the message.

What will be deleted?

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • All data associated with your account (tasks, settings, statistics, etc.)

What will be retained?

No personal data will be retained. Once deleted, your account and all data will be permanently removed from our servers.

When will the deletion be processed?

We will process your account deletion request within 7 days of receiving your email.
